🎫 How To Play GOLDENTICKETLE

Step inside the mysterious sweet factory! Every day, there is a secret 12-LETTER word.

The word is either:

  • GOLDENTICKET (1 in 1000 chance today!)
  • CHOCOLATEBAR (999 in 1000 chance today)

You have exactly 2 CHANCES to enter any 12-letter words and hit ENTER.

After each guess, the tiles will change colors to show how close your letters were to the target:

C
Green / Brown means the letter is correct and in the exact spot.
P
Caramel / Orange means the letter is in the word but in a different spot.
A
Grey means the letter is not in the word at all.

Play once per day. Reset occurs at your local midnight. Good luck!
