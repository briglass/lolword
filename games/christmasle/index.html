🎄 How to Play Christmasle

Welcome to Christmasle, the absolute jolliest holiday word guessing game with an incredibly festive twist!

The secret answer changes daily and is chosen from a special mystery pool of seasonal words. Can you guess today's cheerful holiday secret? Merry Christmas!

  • The length of the secret word changes every day depending on today's secret solution!
  • You have 6 attempts to solve it.
  • To make things simple, any letter combination is allowed as a guess. Enter absolute gibberish if it helps!
  • A new word is delivered every single night at midnight!
